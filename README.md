### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm **David Amorim**, a Full Stack Developer with over 6 years of experience working on medium and large-scale projects. I focus on performance, scalability, clean architecture, and software best practices across both frontend and backend.

I have solid experience with **React**, **Next.js**, **Node.js**, **NestJS**, and microservice architecture. I'm passionate about clean code, automated testing, CI/CD, and user-centered design.

- 🔎 Enthusiast of TDD, clean code and scalable systems
- 👥 Experienced in mentoring and code reviews
- 🌍 Background in frontend, backend, and mobile development
- 🎓 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Education (UFRPE - ongoing)
